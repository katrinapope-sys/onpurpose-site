What is FoodAura?

Nutrition Science Meets Oncology Care

Nutrition is one of the most evidence-supported yet consistently under-addressed components of oncology care. FoodAura bridges that gap — giving both patients and clinicians an intelligent, accessible tool that turns nutritional best practices into real-world action.

Built from clinical insight and lived experience with cancer care, FoodAura is designed to meet patients where they are and support clinicians in delivering better, more holistic care.

  • 🧠

    AI-Powered Personalization

    Tailored nutrition guidance based on diagnosis, treatment phase, side effects, and patient preferences.

  • 👨‍⚕️

    Clinician Dashboard

    Tools for registered dietitians, oncologists, and care coordinators to monitor and guide patient nutrition remotely.

  • 📊

    Evidence-Based Scoring

    Every food recommendation grounded in oncology nutrition research, not generic dietary databases.

  • 🔗

    Integrated Care Workflows

    Designed to complement existing care team workflows, not replace the human connection in care delivery.

Who FoodAura is Built For
For Patients

Cancer patients at any stage of their journey who want to understand what they're eating, how it affects their treatment, and what they can do to nourish themselves better — in language they can actually understand.

For Clinicians

Oncologists, registered dietitians, nurses, and care coordinators who want a better way to track, communicate, and support their patients' nutritional health between visits.

Our Story

OnPurpose Integrative Care was not born in a boardroom. It was born in the spaces between — in the hallways of hospitals, on the tables of exam rooms, during repetitive appointments, the exhaustion of side effects and questions regarding pain no one could account for. OnPurpose Integrative Care exists because the healthcare system, even at its best, leaves gaps. And in those gaps, especially for oncology patients in rural Eastern North Carolina, people suffer without cause.

Our founder created OnPurpose Integrative Care with a singular conviction: that every person navigating a cancer diagnosis deserves care that is not only clinically sound, but human — coordinated, compassionate, and deliberate at every step.

The Intentional Standard of Care

The Intentional Standard of Care is OnPurpose Integrative Care's founding operational doctrine. It defines the non-negotiable expectation that every clinical decision, every patient interaction, and every organizational process be carried out with full deliberateness — not habit, not convenience, not protocol alone. Care at OnPurpose must be chosen, not defaulted to. Every action is evaluated against the question: Is this what we would want for ourselves or someone we love?

Why Eastern North Carolina

Pitt County and the surrounding Eastern NC region face some of the most persistent health disparities in the state. Cancer incidence and mortality rates in rural ENC outpace state and national averages. Access to specialist oncology care, supportive services, and nutrition guidance is severely limited. Transportation, insurance gaps, and social determinants of health compound these challenges daily.

OnPurpose Integrative Care was built for this community — not as a charity, but as a sustainable, high-integrity ecosystem that believes excellent care and rural service are not in conflict.

How We're Structured

OnPurpose Integrative Care operates as a multi-entity integrated care ecosystem, with six specialized service areas designed to cover the full oncology care continuum. This structure enables seamless internal referral, value-based care participation, and maximum impact per patient — while maintaining full regulatory compliance across each licensed entity.
